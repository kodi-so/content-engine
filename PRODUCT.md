## Design Context

### Users
Content Engine is for creators, operators, and founders running agentic content workflows across social accounts. They use it to create one-off content, review generated assets, manage repeatable workflows, and inspect operational state.

### Product Personality
Calm, capable, and editorial. The interface should feel like a creative control room: precise enough for operations, warm enough for content work, and confident enough that users trust the agent.

### Aesthetic Direction
Favor a light, high-legibility application UI with cool tinted neutrals, inky navigation, restrained teal actions, and a rare citron accent for generated/agentic moments. Avoid beige dashboards, purple-blue AI gradients, glassmorphism, generic hero metrics, decorative sparklines, nested cards, and evenly repeated card grids.

### Design Principles
- Prioritize the creation and review workflow over decorative presentation.
- Use typography, spacing, and alignment before color or effects to establish hierarchy.
- Keep operational surfaces dense but calm; users should scan, compare, and act quickly.
- Make generated content previews feel special, but keep controls predictable and accessible.
- Treat mobile as an adapted workflow, not a shrunken desktop.
